•• P 6.6 Compute the alternating sum of all elements in an array. For example, if your program
reads the input
1     4     9      1      6       9          7      4           9         11
then it computes
1 – 4 + 9 – 16 + 9 – 7 + 4 – 9 + 11 = –2
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
public static double sum(double [] values)
	{
		
	double sum=0;
		for(int i=0;i<values.length;i++)
		{
			if(i%2==0)
				sum=sum+values[i];
			else
				sum=sum-values[i];	
		}
		return sum;
		
	}
